EYO Support

If you need help with EYO, have feedback, want to report an issue, or have questions about your subscription, please contact us by email.

Support email: eyo.app.support@gmail.com

Please include your device model, operating system version, app version, and a short description of the issue so we can help you faster.

What we can help with

  • App usage questions;
  • Bug reports or technical issues;
  • Questions about EYO Pro;
  • Subscription or restore purchase questions;
  • Feedback and feature suggestions;
  • Privacy or legal questions.

Subscriptions and purchases

EYO Pro purchases and subscriptions are handled through Apple App Store or Google Play. If you need to cancel, manage, or request a refund for a subscription, please use the subscription settings of the platform where you purchased EYO Pro.
