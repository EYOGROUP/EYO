EYO

One app for work, study, fitness, and home routines.

EYO helps you organize your day across multiple modes: track work time, stay focused while studying, manage workouts, and keep home and kitchen routines under control.

What EYO helps with

Work Track projects, time entries, reports, and billable work.
Study Use focus sessions, Pomodoro timers, subjects, and study statistics.
Fitness Manage workouts, rest timers, warm-ups, progress, and smart training suggestions.
Home & Kitchen Organize tasks, shopping lists, pantry items, recipes, expiry reminders, and kitchen timers.

EYO Pro

EYO Pro may unlock advanced features across all modes, including extended reports, exports, smart suggestions, automations, additional timers, custom sounds, and premium tools.
