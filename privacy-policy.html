EYO Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how EYO (“EYO”, “the App”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use the EYO mobile application, related features, and legal/support pages.

EYO is designed as a local-first productivity and lifestyle app. Most user-generated content is stored locally on your device and is not uploaded to an EYO cloud account by default.

In the current version, EYO does not provide general cloud synchronization for user-generated app content unless a specific feature clearly states otherwise.

1. Overview

EYO is a multi-mode productivity and lifestyle application designed to help users organize time, routines, tasks, focus sessions, workouts, home and kitchen activities, timers, reminders, reports, and personal productivity workflows.

Depending on the selected mode and app version, EYO may include:

  • Work and freelance time tracking;
  • Projects, time entries, reports, exports, and productivity summaries;
  • Study focus sessions, Pomodoro timers, subjects, study statistics, and learning routines;
  • Fitness workouts, rest timers, workout templates, warm-ups, progress tracking, and smart coach suggestions;
  • Home and kitchen tasks, shopping lists, pantry tracking, expiry reminders, recipes, meal planning, and kitchen timers;
  • Notifications, timer alerts, reminders, alarm sounds, and custom sound settings;
  • Free and paid Pro features.

2. Local-First Data Storage

Most user-generated data in EYO is stored locally on your device. Depending on the features you use, this may include:

  • Projects, client names entered by the user, hourly rates, and time entries;
  • Start and end times, durations, notes, billable and non-billable status;
  • Reports generated from local entries;
  • Study subjects, focus sessions, Pomodoro settings, study goals, and study statistics;
  • Fitness workouts, exercises, sets, repetitions, weights, rest timers, templates, warm-ups, and progress data;
  • Home and kitchen tasks, shopping lists, pantry items, expiry dates, recipes, meal plans, and kitchen timers;
  • Notification settings, alarm sound settings, imported custom sounds, and app preferences;
  • Selected app mode, theme, language, and local settings;
  • Local Pro status or purchase-related state used to unlock app features.

By default, this user-generated content is not uploaded to an EYO server.

3. No Account Required

EYO can currently be used without creating a user account. The current version does not require email-based registration, login credentials, or a cloud user profile for normal app usage.

If account-based or cloud synchronization features are introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy may be updated accordingly.

4. Data We May Collect

EYO may process different categories of information depending on how you use the App:

4.1 Data You Enter Into the App

You may enter information such as projects, time entries, notes, study subjects, focus sessions, workouts, exercises, recipes, shopping items, pantry items, expiry dates, timers, goals, preferences, and other app content.

This information is currently stored primarily locally on your device.

4.2 App Settings and Preferences

EYO may store settings such as selected language, theme, selected app mode, timer settings, sound preferences, notification preferences, Pro-related settings, and other configuration data.

4.3 Imported Custom Sounds

If you import custom alarm sounds, such as MP3 or WAV files, these files are intended to be stored locally on your device and used for in-app alarm or timer functionality.

4.4 Analytics Data

EYO may use Firebase Analytics, a service provided by Google Firebase, to understand general app usage and improve the App.

Firebase Analytics may collect information such as app interactions, feature usage, app version, device type, operating system version, approximate region, session information, and other technical or usage events.

This information is used to understand how the App is used, improve usability, improve performance, and prioritize future development.

EYO does not use Firebase Analytics to sell user data or display third-party advertising inside the App.

4.5 Crash and Diagnostic Data

If crash reporting or diagnostic tools are enabled in a current or future version, technical crash or diagnostic data may be processed to identify bugs, improve stability, and maintain reliability.

If such tools are not enabled in a specific app version, this section may not apply to that version.

5. Firebase Analytics

EYO may use Firebase Analytics to collect limited technical and usage information for app improvement purposes.

Firebase Analytics may process data such as:

  • App launches and session information;
  • General feature usage events;
  • Device type and operating system version;
  • App version and platform;
  • Approximate location or region derived from technical data;
  • Interaction events used to understand app performance and usage.

EYO uses this information to improve the App, understand which features are useful, identify usability issues, and support product development.

On Android, Firebase Analytics may use device identifiers or advertising identifiers where available and permitted by the platform for analytics and attribution purposes. EYO does not use this information to display third-party ads or sell user data.

6. Supabase and Internal Administration

EYO may use Supabase for limited internal administration or app-management functionality.

Supabase is not currently used as a general cloud database for storing all user-generated content created inside the App.

User-generated app content such as projects, time entries, study sessions, fitness data, home and kitchen tasks, recipes, shopping lists, pantry items, and custom sounds is currently intended to remain stored locally on the user’s device unless a specific feature clearly states otherwise.

7. Pro Access and In-App Purchases

Some EYO features may be limited to Pro users. EYO Pro may unlock features across multiple modes, including Work, Study, Fitness, and Home & Kitchen.

Pro access may be provided through official in-app purchases or subscriptions offered by Apple App Store or Google Play.

Payment processing, subscription management, cancellations, and refunds are handled by the relevant app store provider. EYO does not store full payment card details.

EYO may store purchase status locally on the device and may use app store purchase information to determine whether Pro features should be available.

8. Notifications, Reminders, and Timer Alerts

EYO may request notification permission to show timer-related notifications, reminders, focus alerts, break alerts, fitness rest alerts, home and kitchen timer alerts, task reminders, expiry reminders, or other local alerts.

Notifications are intended to be generated locally or through the device’s notification system, depending on the feature and platform.

You can enable or disable notifications through the App settings or your device settings.

Notification delivery may depend on device permissions, operating system behavior, battery optimization, focus mode, silent mode, app state, and platform restrictions.

9. CSV, PDF, Reports, and Exports

EYO may allow users to export data as CSV, PDF, or other report formats.

Exports may include user-entered or locally stored data, such as project names, client names, time entry dates, start and end times, durations, notes, billable status, hourly rates, calculated values, summaries, study information, fitness information, or other report data, depending on the feature.

Export files are created only when the user chooses to generate, export, save, or share them.

Once you export or share a file outside EYO, that file may be handled by other apps, operating system services, cloud providers, email providers, or recipients selected by you.

10. Data Sharing

EYO does not sell user data.

EYO does not use third-party advertising networks and does not share user-generated app data for third-party advertising purposes.

EYO may share limited technical, analytics, purchase, or operational data with service providers where necessary to provide, maintain, improve, or analyze the App.

Service providers may include:

  • Google Firebase for analytics and app usage measurement;
  • Apple App Store and Google Play for in-app purchases and subscriptions;
  • Supabase for limited internal administration or app-management functionality;
  • Operating system services used for sharing, notifications, storage, or app functionality.

The user may choose to manually export or share data, for example by creating a CSV or PDF export, sharing a report, sending a file, or using the device share sheet.

11. Cloud Sync and Backup

The current version does not include general cloud sync by default.

The current version may not include automatic backup or restore features for all user data. Users are responsible for keeping any exported files or important data safe.

Device-level backup systems, such as iCloud backup or Android backup, may affect app data depending on platform settings and operating system behavior.

12. Data Deletion

Because EYO stores most user-generated data locally by default, users can delete app data by deleting entries, projects, sessions, workouts, tasks, recipes, pantry items, settings, or other content where supported by the App.

Users may also delete local app data by removing the App from the device, subject to the operating system’s storage and backup behavior.

Analytics data processed through Firebase is handled according to Google Firebase’s data processing and retention practices.

If you contact us for privacy-related questions, we may ask for information necessary to identify and handle your request.

13. Data Retention

User-generated data stored locally remains on your device until you delete it, delete the App, reset the device, or otherwise remove the data through device or app functionality.

Analytics data may be retained by Firebase or related service providers according to their own retention settings and policies.

Support emails or privacy-related requests may be retained for as long as necessary to respond to your request, maintain records, prevent abuse, or comply with legal obligations.

14. Children

EYO is not designed for children and is intended for productivity, study, fitness, and personal organization use.

EYO is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for digital services consent. If you believe that a child has provided personal data through EYO, please contact us.

15. International Data Transfers

Service providers such as Google Firebase, Apple, Google Play, or Supabase may process data in countries outside your country of residence.

Where applicable, such processing is subject to the service providers’ own data protection terms, safeguards, and policies.

16. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal data, such as the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing.

Because most user-generated app data is stored locally on your device, you can manage or delete much of this data directly in the App or by deleting the App from your device.

You can manage notification permissions through your device settings.

You can manage subscriptions through Apple App Store or Google Play subscription settings.

For privacy-related requests, contact us using the email address listed below.

17. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures designed to protect information processed by EYO.

However, no method of storage, transmission, or processing is completely secure. You are responsible for protecting access to your device, operating system account, app store account, exported files, and backups.

18. Third-Party Links and Services

EYO may contain links to external pages, such as the Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, Support page, or app store pages.

Third-party services are governed by their own privacy policies and terms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

19. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as EYO evolves. Changes may be required when new features are added, such as account creation, cloud sync, new analytics tools, new integrations, additional subscription options, or new app modes.

When we update this Privacy Policy, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of EYO after changes means that you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
